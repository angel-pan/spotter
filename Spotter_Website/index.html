Our Mission

With Spotter, we aim to empower beginners at the gym. There are many people that are motivated to start going their fitness journey but feel intimidated by experienced gym-goers and are afraid to ask for help. Our solution is to provide a quick and easy way for beginners at the gym to see where available help is.

Needfinding

During initial interviews, we explored what wellness means to professional athletes, experienced gym-goers, and beginners starting out on their fitness journey. These interviews informed our decision to focus on helping beginners start a physical fitness regimen.
